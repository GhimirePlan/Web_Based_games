HOW IT WORKS

Snake Game working steps

Control the Snake

Use the arrow keys to navigate the snake on the screen, guiding it towards food items to grow in length.

Collect and Avoid

Eat the food items that appear randomly on the screen to earn points and increase the snake's length. Avoid running into the snake's own body or the walls to continue playing.

Score and Compete

The game tracks your score based on the number of food items collected. Aim to achieve the highest score possible before the snake collides with itself or the screen edges, ending the game.
